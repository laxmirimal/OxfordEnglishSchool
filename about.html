About Oxford English School

Our History

Established in 2055 B.S., Oxford English School started with a humble vision: to provide exceptional, accessible education to the children of Damak. Over the decades, we have grown into a premier institution, known for our academic rigor and commitment to student development.

We believe in a balanced approach to education, blending traditional values with modern teaching methodologies to prepare our students for the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Mission

To empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to thrive in a global community. We aim to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong passion for learning in a safe and supportive environment.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Nepal, recognized for academic excellence, innovative teaching, and the holistic development of every student, preparing them to be responsible and compassionate global citizens.

Our Features

Expert Faculty

Our dedicated teachers are experts in their fields, committed to nurturing each student's potential.


Digital Learning

We utilize smart boards and digital resources to create an interactive and engaging learning atmosphere.


Science Labs

Fully-equipped physics, chemistry, and biology labs for hands-on practical learning & research.


Rich Library

A vast collection of books, journals, and digital media to support research and reading.


Transport Facility

Safe and reliable bus services covering various routes across the city and surrounding areas.


Sports & ECA

Ample opportunities for students to excel in sports and other extracurricular activities.
