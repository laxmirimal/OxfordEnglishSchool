Admission Process

We welcome applications from students for classes Playgroup to Class X. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and straightforward.

Steps to Apply:

  1. Fill out the online inquiry form (below, sent via WhatsApp).
  2. Visit the school to collect the admission packet.
  3. Submit the completed application form with documents.
  4. Appear for a small entrance test (for Class I and above).
  5. Interview with the student and parents.
  6. Confirmation of admission and fee payment.

Available Classes:

  • Playgroup (PG)
  • Nursery, LKG, UKG
  • Class I to Class X
