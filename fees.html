Fee Structure

We are committed to providing a transparent and fair fee structure. Our fees are structured in accordance with the guidelines set by educational authorities in Nepal.

Fee Information Notice

For the latest and most detailed fee structure, including term-specific and optional charges, please **contact the school administration office directly**.

Other Fees & Services (Optional)

Transportation

Optional bus service is available. Fees vary based on the route and distance.

Uniforms & Books

Available for purchase from the school's designated counter before the session begins.

Lunch / Canteen

Hygienic day-time meals are available at the canteen. This is an optional service.

Payment Guidelines

  • All monthly fees must be paid within the **10th of each Nepali month**.
  • A late fee will be applicable for payments made after the due date.
  • Payments can be made directly at the school's Account Section or via Bank/eSewa.
  • Please retain all payment receipts for your records.
